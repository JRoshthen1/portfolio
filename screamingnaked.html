More about this project


Their landing page was this "cool 3D space" where you could (sometimes couldn't) navigate by moving your cursor. From multimedia designer's point of view the page had big problems with user interaction and overall flow. Even us, young and computer proficient group of people had hard time finding some information about the band itself. The page did not work on mobile devices at all. Their social media was something around 60 followers on Instagram and more than 100 likes on their facebook page. We helped them out by creating brand new visual identity corresponding with their (at the time) new album. With that, creating a new website but sadly, they decided to simplify it but this is what I created. The main inspiration for this style was the good old Space Jam Website. For the SoMe, we have made strategy for 2 months which they stopped following after couple of weeks but during that time they gained more than 200 followers! And last but not least, the music video, we did a lot of competitive analysis for brainsotrming this one. Just take a look ;)
